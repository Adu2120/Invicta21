 A Storytelling   workshop

About the workshop

A one day workshop, in which I have included the following points:

  • What is story telling
  • Types of story telling
  • Common Plots used in Story Telling
  • How to Prepare before A Stage act
  • Voice Modulations
  • Character Understanding
  • Use of Story in effective communication!

More about workshop:

There will be many Activities which will be conducted for a better understanding of the above topics and to know whether the participants are able to use the skills in real life situations.

Duration: 1 Day
Date: 3rd July 2021
Time: 10:00 AM- 1:00 PM

What you will get to learn from this workshop?

The expected out come is, the participants will have a better idea of storytelling, they will be able to decide which story telling type they are good in and which plot they are comfortable with, also they will be able to use story telling as an effective communication aid and will be able to perform well on a stage.
